Emotional Intelligence

Today’s professional skills lecture about Emotional Intelligence.At first we were talked about Definition and Importance of Emotion,
Emotions: feelings, associated thoughts, psychological and biological states, and behaviours. Primary and Secondary Emotions: anger, sadness, fear, enjoyment, love, surprise, disgust, shame

  • What is Emotional Intelligence (EI)
  • IQ vs. EQ
  • Emotional Intelligence and Leadership

What is Emotional Intelligence (EI)?
Defined by Salovey and Mayer (1990) as the ability to monitor and manage one's own and others' emotions Key skills: perceiving emotions, understanding emotional meanings, using emotions in thinking, managing emotions.


IQ vs. EQ
IQ measures cognitive abilities; EQ measures emotional understanding and management Success relies on both IQ and EQ, with EQ being crucial for managing stress, building relationships, and effective leadership.Next we were discuss about how Developing Emotional Intelligence

Self-awareness: Recognizing and evaluating one's own emotions
Self-regulation: Controlling and expressing emotions appropriately
Strategies: mindfulness, cognitive re-appraisal, acceptance, problem-solving
Motivation: Pursuing goals and persevering through setbacks Emotionally
intelligent people are intrinsically motivated and optimistic
Empathy: Understanding and responding to others' emotions
Techniques: recognizing biases, setting aside personal feelings, being attentive to cues
Social Skills: Interacting and cooperating with others
Skills: influence, communication, managing change, conflict resolution, building bonds, collaboration

Emotional Intelligence and Leadership

Effective leadership requires understanding and responding to others emotions Goleman's Emotional Competencies: self-awareness, self-regulation,motivation, empathy, social skills

Practical Applications

EI helps in reducing stress, defusing conflicts, enhancing job satisfaction, and making informed decisions It fosters kindness and connection with inner feelings, important for personal and professional growth





Finally I understand By mastering emotional intelligence, individuals can improve their relationships, succeed in their careers, and achieve personal goals. So with that todays lecture was ended
