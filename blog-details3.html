What are the uses of a portfolio ?
We started this lesson by talking about the main goals of a portfolio. Therefore, we spoke about a few main usages of using a portfolio. We can list a few examples, including Job Applications,Career Advancement, Creative Industries, Educational Assessments, Educational Admissions, Teaching and Education, Freelancing and Consulting, Professional Development, Performance Evaluation, Skill Demonstrations, Entrepreneurship, Networking and Personal Branding.


To put it another way, portfolios are frequently used as a supplement to resumes, showing results, projects, and work samples that highlight a candidate's background and skills .it's beneficial for job applications. When applying for new chances inside their current organization or for promotions, increases, or other opportunities, professionals can use portfolios to showcase their experiences, successes, and talents.

Then we discussed about the types of the portfolios.

1. Personal Portfolio
A personal portfolio is a collection of creative work, achievements, and projects that showcase skills, talents, and interests.

2. Career/Dossier Portfolio
A career or dossier portfolio is designed to present professional achievements and credentials.It typically includes resumes, cover letters, reference letters, and documentation of work experience.

3. Employment Portfolio
An employment portfolio is a comprehensive collection of work-related documents, accomplishments, and evidence of skills

4. Assessment Portfolio
An assessment portfolio is used to showcase learning progress, growth, and achievements over a period of time.
