Interview Skills

Today’s professional skills lecture about Interview Skills. below I write down some of details about today lesson. firstly we discuss about what is the job interview “A job interview is a conversation between an job applicant and a representative of the organization that is looking forward to hiring the interviewee. Everyone involved in a job interview has a common purpose.” here we discuss about,

  • Interview strategy
  • ESTABLISH CLEAR OBJECTIVES
  • NEGOTIATE EFFECTIVELY

lecturer told us an effective communication is also very important when we going to face an interview there are some skills used at job interview those are,

  • non-verbal communication
  • listening
  • responding
  • questioning


Next we talked about the role of non-verbal communication non verbal communication is also important for job interviews I write down some points I was identify in that moment,

  • 7% of what we say is communicated by our words.
  • 38% of what we say is communicated by our tone of voice
  • 55% of our message we say is communicated by our body language.
  • 55% of our message we say is communicated by our body language.
  • Therefore, 93% of face-to-face communication is via non-verbal channels.


All above things are important when we going to face an interview and also Active listening very important when we going to face an interview when we at interview we must need to,

  • Pay attention
  • Show that you are listening
  • Seek clarification if you don’t understand
  • Deter judgment.
  • Respond appropriately.


6 C’S OF EFFECTIVE COMMUNICATION



1. CLEAR Ensure that information is presented clearly.

2. CONCISE Do not lose the message by being long-winded.

3. CORRECT Avoid giving misleading information.

4. COMPLETE Give all the necessary information and not just part of it

5. CONCRETE Give examples and stories rather than talking about things in general.

6. COURTEOUS Always be and sound polite.





And then our lecturer told us we must get language support when participating an interview below I breakdown some of details I gathered

  • Describing our personality: adjectives.
  • Describing our strengths: present tenses.
  • Describing our education and experience : past simple and present perfect.
  • Describing our future goals.
  • Solving common pronunciation problems.
